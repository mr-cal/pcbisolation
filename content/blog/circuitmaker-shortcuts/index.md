Here's a list of the most useful shortcuts in CM - more graphical and intuitive than the built in shortcut list (Ribbon Bar->View->Shortcuts).

![shortcut_link](shortcut_link.jpg)_Above - Shortcut List Location_

Pro Tip: When the tools aren't working correctly, press ESC a few times.

# Schematic Shortcuts

Crtl+Scroll | Zoom |   
---|---|---  
Center Button - Click Hold and Drag | Zoom |   
Pg Up/Down | Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Right Button - Click Hold Drag | Pan |   
Ctrl+A | Select **A** ll |  ![Sch+Ctrl+A](SchCtrlA.jpg)  
Crtl+C | **C** opy |

Ctrl+V | Paste |   
Ctrl+D | **D** uplicate |  ![Sch+Ctrl+D](SchCtrlD.jpg)  
W | **W** ire (for opening wiring tool) |  ![Sch+Ctrl+W](SchW.jpg)  
Space | Change Wiring Path - Only works when wiring |  ![Sch+Space2](SchSpace2.jpg)  
Shift+Space | Change Wiring Angles - Only works when wiring |  ![Sch+Shift+Space3](SchShiftSpace3.jpg)  
Space | Rotate Selected Object |  ![Sch+Rotate](SchRotate.jpg)  
Tab or Double Click | Edit Properties of selected object |  ![Sch+Tab](SchTab.jpg)  
G | Toggle **G** rid Size (look in bottom left-hand corner) |  ![Sch+G](SchG.jpg)  
Z | Add Ground |  ![Sch+Z](SchZ.jpg)

# PCB Shortcuts

Crtl+Scroll | Zoom |   
---|---|---  
Center Button - Click Hold and Drag | Zoom |   
Pg Up/Down | Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Shift+Pg Up/Down | Precise Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Right Button - Click Hold Drag | Pan |   
Esc | Exit Tool (so very useful) |   
Ctrl+A | Select All |  ![PCB+Ctrl+A](PCBCtrlA.jpg)  
Crtl+C | Copy |   
Ctrl+X | Cut |   
Ctrl+V | Paste |   
R | Route Trace |  ![PCB+R](PCBR.jpg)  
Space | Change Routing Path - Only works when routing |  ![PCB+Space2](PCBSpace2.jpg)  
Shift+Space | Change Routing Angles - Only works when routing |  ![PCB+Shift+Space2](PCBShiftSpace2.jpg)  
Space | Rotate Selected Object |   
G | Toggle Grid Size (look in bottom left-hand corner) |  ![PCB+G](PCBG.jpg)  
Shift+S | Single Layer View (Toggle visibility of all layers except for the active layer) |  ![PCB+Shift+S3](PCBShiftS3.jpg)  
Backspace | Undo Trace Segements of selected trace |  ![PCB+Backspace3](PCBBackspace3.jpg)  
Tab or Double Click | Edit Properties of selected object |  ![PCB+Tab](PCBTab.jpg)
