Here's a list of the most useful shortcuts in CM - more graphical and intuitive than the built in shortcut list (Ribbon Bar->View->Shortcuts).

[![shortcut_link](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/shortcut_link-300x158.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/shortcut_link.jpg>)_Above - Shortcut List Location_

Pro Tip: When the tools aren't working correctly, press ESC a few times.

# Schematic Shortcuts

Crtl+Scroll | Zoom |   
---|---|---  
Center Button - Click Hold and Drag | Zoom |   
Pg Up/Down | Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Right Button - Click Hold Drag | Pan |   
Ctrl+A | Select **A** ll |  [![Sch+Ctrl+A](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCtrlA-300x135.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCtrlA.jpg>)  
Crtl+C | **C** opy |   
Ctrl+X | Cut |   
Ctrl+V | Paste |   
Ctrl+D | **D** uplicate |  [![Sch+Ctrl+D](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCtrlD-300x220.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCtrlD.jpg>)  
W | **W** ire (for opening wiring tool) |  [![Sch+Ctrl+W](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchW-300x183.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchW.jpg>)  
Space | Change Wiring Path - Only works when wiring |  [![Sch+Space2](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchSpace2-300x109.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchSpace2.jpg>)  
Shift+Space | Change Wiring Angles - Only works when wiring |  [![Sch+Shift+Space3](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchShiftSpace3-300x89.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchShiftSpace3.jpg>)  
Space | Rotate Selected Object |  [![Sch+Rotate](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchRotate-300x203.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchRotate.jpg>)  
Tab or Double Click | Edit Properties of selected object |  [![Sch+Tab](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchTab-300x147.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchTab.jpg>)  
G | Toggle **G** rid Size (look in bottom left-hand corner) |  [![Sch+G](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchG.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchG.jpg>)  
Z | Add Ground |  [![Sch+Z](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchZ-300x220.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchZ.jpg>)  
V | Add **V** CC (press TAB to quickly change net name) |  [![Sch+V](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchV-300x242.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchV.jpg>)  
T | **T** ext (press TAB to change text) |  [![Sch+T](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchT-300x212.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchT.jpg>)  
N | **N** et Label (press TAB to change text) |  [![Sch+N](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchN-300x262.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchN.jpg>)  
C | Place **C** omponent |  [![Sch+C](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchC-300x273.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchC.jpg>)  
X | Flip left/right (**X** -Axis) |   
Y | Flip up/down (**Y** -Axis) |   
Esc | **E** xit Tool |   
Ctrl+Tab | Go to Next Tab |  [![Sch+Crtl+Tab](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCrtlTab-300x35.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCrtlTab.jpg>)  
Ctrl+Shift+Tab | Go to Previous Tab |  [![Sch+Crtl+Tab](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCrtlTab-300x35.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/SchCrtlTab.jpg>)  
  
 

 

# PCB Shortcuts

Crtl+Scroll | Zoom |   
---|---|---  
Center Button - Click Hold and Drag | Zoom |   
Pg Up/Down | Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Shift+Pg Up/Down | Precise Zoom (Great for laptops) |   
Right Button - Click Hold Drag | Pan |   
Esc | Exit Tool (so very useful) |   
Ctrl+A | Select All |  [![PCB+Ctrl+A](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBCtrlA-300x194.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBCtrlA.jpg>)  
Crtl+C | Copy |   
Ctrl+X | Cut |   
Ctrl+V | Paste |   
R | Route Trace |  [![PCB+R](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBR-300x182.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBR.jpg>)  
Space | Change Routing Path - Only works when routing |  [![PCB+Space2](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBSpace2-300x90.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBSpace2.jpg>)  
Shift+Space | Change Routing Angles - Only works when routing |  [![PCB+Shift+Space2](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBShiftSpace2-300x88.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBShiftSpace2.jpg>)  
Space | Rotate Selected Object |   
G | Toggle Grid Size (look in bottom left-hand corner) |  [![PCB+G](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBG-300x80.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBG.jpg>)  
Shift+S | Single Layer View (Toggle visibility of all layers except for the active layer) |  [![PCB+Shift+S3](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBShiftS3-300x94.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBShiftS3.jpg>)  
Backspace | Undo Trace Segements of selected trace |  [![PCB+Backspace3](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBBackspace3-300x91.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBBackspace3.jpg>)  
Tab or Double Click | Edit Properties of selected object |  [![PCB+Tab](/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBTab-300x204.jpg)](</wp-content/uploads/2016/07/PCBTab.jpg>)  
  
 

Have a favorite not listed? Leave it in the comments below.
