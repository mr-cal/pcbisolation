You can see the highlights of Vientiane, Laos in one long day.

A scooter will be the cheapest way if you want to see the downtown sights + Buddha park.

A bicycle will allow you to see everything in 5 hours.

Walking is a great option as well, as you can use a tuktuk at the end of the day!

### Sights

**A** - [Sisaket Temple](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wat_Si_Saket>)

**B** - [That Dam](<https://www.renown-travel.com/laos/temples/that-dam.html>)

**C** - [Patuxay Monument ](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Patuxai>)

Inspired by the [Arc de Triomphe](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arc_de_Triomphe>)

**D** - [Pha That Luang](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pha_That_Luang>)

National icon of Laos

**E** - [COPE Visitor Centre](<http://www.copelaos.org/visit.php>)

A must see!

**F** - [Wat Sok Pa Luang](<http://www.lonelyplanet.com/laos/vientiane/activities/health-wellness/wat-sok-pa-luang>)

Daily meditations, massages, and sauna nearby!

**G** - [Buddha Park](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buddha_Park>)

**H** - [Night Market](<http://www.visit-laos.com/vientiane/shopping/riverside-night-market.htm>)
